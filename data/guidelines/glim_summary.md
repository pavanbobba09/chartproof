# GLIM malnutrition criteria summary (stretch goal)

source_id: glim_summary

Educational summary for a future malnutrition criteria file. Not implemented in v1. Not for clinical use.

## Section: Phenotypic and etiologic criteria

GLIM requires at least one phenotypic criterion (weight loss, low BMI, or reduced muscle mass) and one etiologic criterion (reduced intake/assimilation or inflammation/disease burden).
