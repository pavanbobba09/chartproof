# KDIGO AKI criteria summary (stretch goal)

source_id: kdigo_aki_summary

Educational summary for a future AKI criteria file. Not implemented in v1. Not for clinical use.

## Section: Creatinine and urine output

KDIGO defines AKI stages using creatinine rise (absolute or relative) and/or reduced urine output over defined time windows. ChartProof may later encode a simplified creatinine-rise rule similar to the sepsis organ-dysfunction child.
